About Us


SeedBase is a start-up for all early stage start-ups and entrepreneurs, based in National Capital (Ottawa), Canada. We are the design service platform for all early-stage start-ups and entrepreneurs to find affordable engineering design services to form and grow their business.

Our clients, early stage start-ups and entrepreneurs, are now only a few clicks away from highly skilled freelancers and highly experienced engineer residents, who will develop their great idea, product or service at an affordable cost.
