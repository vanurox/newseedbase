How do I choose among all the right projects to mentor?

Create a resident profile and tailor mentorship request to the Specific category, and time-frame you only wish to mentor. Choose among options to make your profile public or not. This will allow for flexibility with receiving project Requests. Search the Business board for ideas, projects or Businesses receiving attention. Ensure to clearly specify the skill required and location (if Applicable) within application. More for finding resident, Clearly state the stage and duration of advice required.

How do I grow a Mentorship Profile?

Make your profile visible to improve accessibility to available projects Join the conversations on business board and makers club. Provide recommendations to students to validate their freelance profiles
