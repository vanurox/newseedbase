How do I get hired by a Client?

Sign up and create a basic profile Make sure your profile is compelling. A compelling profile is -represents your strongest maker or creative skill/talent -meets with the skill/talent request of the project applied for Search jobs within the job board or project category of interest Hit the Apply button and fill in the short application form

How do I build UP?

Find skills building modules within our Resource Inventory to Improve your performance on projects Target specific skills building modules which you wish to build Request recommendations from residents to add to profile. This Also improves your chances of becoming a featured freelancers. Featured Freelancers can send out proposals at higher negotiable rates Improve your profile with recognised skills certifications

How do I get paid?

Payments are made securely through SeedBase platform You will receive your payment following instructions finalised In negotiations with the Client. i.e. via Paypal and per; -Milestones or -20/80 payment (this is 20% upon commencement and 80% Upon successful completion)

Other payment options may include

  • - Volunteer service
  • - Equity/Commission/Royalty
  • - Retainer agreement
